Title: Your Article Title Here (Maximum 280 characters)

Summary: This is where you add your article summary. The summary should be concise and give readers a clear idea of what the article is about. Keep it under 100 words. This will appear on the home page and in the news listing.

Article: This is the main content of your article. It should be between 500 and 2000 words.

The article content should be well-structured with proper paragraphs. Separate paragraphs with blank lines.

You can format your article with basic formatting:

For a new paragraph, simply leave a blank line between paragraphs.

For emphasis, you can use *asterisks* or _underscores_, and the system will convert these to HTML formatting.

For headings within your article, you can use the following format:
## Secondary Heading
### Tertiary Heading

For lists, you can use the following formats:

1. First item
2. Second item
3. Third item

Or for unordered lists:

- First bullet point
- Second bullet point
- Third bullet point

For blockquotes, use the > symbol:

> This is a blockquote that will be formatted specially in the final article.

When your article is processed, it will be automatically added to the website:
1. The title and summary will appear in the Latest News section on the home page
2. The article will be added to the News page
3. A dedicated page will be created for the full article content

Note: Images are not supported in the automatic upload. If you need to include images, please contact the web administrator.
