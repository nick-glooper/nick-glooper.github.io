ALERT: £1bn Public Contract Chaos as ISG Collapses - New Procurement Act's FVRA Framework Could Prevent Your Next Supplier Disaster

ISG's sudden collapse in September 2024 left £1 billion in public contracts stranded and 2,200 workers jobless. The new Procurement Act 2023, implemented February 2025, introduces enhanced Economic and Financial Standing (EFS) requirements that could have flagged ISG's deteriorating position. Through Financial and Value-for-Money Risk Assessments (FVRA), authorities can now utilize forward-looking metrics like Cash Flow to Debt Service Ratio alongside mandatory KPIs.
