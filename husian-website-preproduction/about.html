About Us

Expertise in procurement, commercial management, and AI implementation for public sector organizations.

Our Approach

Husian is a specialized consultancy focused on transforming procurement and commercial operations through the strategic implementation of artificial intelligence. We work exclusively with public sector and defense organizations to enhance their commercial capabilities while ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

Our approach combines deep domain expertise in procurement and commercial management with cutting-edge knowledge of AI applications. We believe that the most effective solutions are those that align technology with organizational objectives, regulatory requirements, and operational realities.

Our Mission

To empower public sector organizations to achieve procurement excellence through the strategic implementation of AI technologies, delivering enhanced value, compliance, and operational efficiency.

Our Expertise

With over 25 years of experience working with UK Central Government departments, defense organizations, and international bodies, we bring specialized expertise in:

Public Sector Procurement

Deep understanding of public sector procurement regulations, processes, and challenges, including the new UK Procurement Act implications.

Commercial Management

Expertise in complex contract management, supplier relationship management, and commercial strategy development for high-value contracts.

AI Implementation

Practical experience implementing AI solutions in procurement and commercial processes, with a focus on measurable outcomes and compliance.

Defense & Security

Specialized knowledge of defense procurement processes, NATO requirements, and security considerations in commercial operations.

Experience & Credentials

Our consultancy is built on a foundation of practical experience working with high-profile public sector organizations:

  • NATO cyber security contract management and commercial advisory services
  • UK Home Office digital data and technology services procurement
  • Ministry of Justice commercial process optimization
  • Implementation of AI-powered contract analysis for central government departments
  • Development of dynamic purchasing systems for public sector organizations
  • Supply chain risk assessment for critical national infrastructure providers

Our Values

Working With Us
